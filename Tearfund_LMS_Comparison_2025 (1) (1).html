🏆 Recommended Choice: Totara Learn + Engage

Best overall fit for Tearfund's compliance requirements, charity sector experience, and cost-effectiveness. Excellent balance of mandatory training enforcement with modern engagement features.

💡 Key Insights About Each Platform:

  • Totara: Purpose-built for complex organizations. Best compliance and charity pricing. Requires implementation partner but offers long-term flexibility and no per-user fees.
  • ELB Learning (Rockstar): Exceptional authoring tools and gamification. Great for organizations that create lots of custom content. Easier implementation than Totara. Strong compliance features but less mature than Totara for complex hierarchies.
  • Degreed: Best user experience and skills mapping. Premium pricing but incredible learner engagement. Consider if user adoption is your #1 priority and budget allows.
  • Cornerstone: Enterprise powerhouse with extensive features. Very strong compliance and HRIS integration. Good for large-scale deployments. Mid-range pricing for nonprofits.
